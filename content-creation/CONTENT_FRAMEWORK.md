- Controversial or surprising fact

- Why should they care?

## üöÄ Your Writing Rules Applied

### ‚úÖ Simple English (A2/B1)
- Short sentences (max 10-12 words)
- Common words only
- Easy pronunciation for non-native speakers

### ‚úÖ Human Vibe Phrases
- "What I personally like about this..."
- "Here is my strategy for..."
- "I honestly think..."
- "This is how I do it..."
- "My approach is different..."

### ‚úÖ No Marketing Fluff
- Replace "game-changer" ‚Üí "saved me 2 hours daily"
- Replace "revolutionary" ‚Üí "works better than X"
- Replace "amazing" ‚Üí specific benefit

### ‚úÖ Dense Value
- Every sentence = fact, number, feature, or opinion
- Remove: "So", "Well", "Actually", "Basically"
- Keep: Specific examples, personal experience

## üìù Content Preferences (Learned from Edits)

### Problem Section
- Focus on time waste: "wastes half of working time" vs "wastes 2 hours daily"
- Be more relatable and impactful

### Tips Structure
- **Explain the WHY**: Not just what to do, but why it works
- **Add specific details**: e.g., "/feature-pipeline starts multiple phases with well-description"
- **Connect to audience**: Mention GitHub for pre-built skills, especially for non-developers
- **Include shocking stats**: "99% of vibe coders do not know about this"
- **Bridge to your content**: Reference Telegram channel for resources

### CTA Strategy
- **Offer multiple options** to choose from based on video goals:
  - **Option A**: Drive to Telegram/external resource
  - **Option B**: Standard follow + engagement
  - **Option C**: Action-based (try specific tip)
- Match text overlay to CTA type
